About John Akwei, Senior Data Scientist ECMp ERMp

John Akwei, Senior Data Scientist ECMp ERMp is the founder of ContextBase, a data science consultancy since January 2013. He has over 20 years of experience as a data initiative leader, assisting data efforts for AIIM International from 2008-2012 and previously working as an administrative assistant at Myvesta from 1999-2002.

He studied Data Science and earned a Data Science Specialization Certificate from Johns Hopkins University in 2014-2015. His skills include formulating hypotheses, statistical programming, building models, experimental design, and statistical inference. Earlier in his career, John Akwei studied electronics technology at Capitol Technology University, graduating in 1986. He also studied computer science at Montgomery College in 1982-1983, and Computer Math at Northwood High School in Wheaton, Maryland, graduating in 1981.

He has published widely on Data Science topics and has proven abilities in full-cycle data science projects. This includes defining business objectives, designing research methods, analyzing data, and presenting findings. He has knowledge of advanced statistical techniques like regression and distributions and has created predictive models, documenting and testing them.

His data science accomplishments include publishing with Analytics Vidhya, posting leading R solutions on Kaggle, and completing client projects utilizing machine learning, predictive analytics, KNN analysis, NLP, web scraping, and more. He has innovated in areas like extreme value theory, topic modeling, spatiotemporal regression, cross-tabulation, and decision mining.

For over 20 years, he has worked in office settings, helping nonprofits in Washington D.C. optimize data for CRM and websites. He follows the CRISP-DM methodology and analyzes SWOT factors in projects. He has skills in taxonomy, metadata, and evaluating model accuracy. John Akwei has a proven ability to meet deadlines, completing over 90 major data science projects.

John Akwei has an extensive publication record that demonstrates his expertise in data science, physics, business applications of AI, and other technical fields. He has been published by Analytics Vidhya, and Noteworthy. His published works cover a wide range of topics.

In the data science realm, he has authored publications on machine learning, time series analysis, topic modeling, cryptocurrency analysis, augmenting R with Tableau, and possibilities of activity stream computing. These show his proficiency in advanced data science methodologies and ability to innovate in applying them to new domains.

He has also published extensively on physics topics, including papers on the Binary Planck Wave/Object/Gravity Theory of the universe's origins and a work examining the Higgs boson's role in unifying the four fundamental forces. These publications reveal his deep knowledge of theoretical physics and cosmology.

Additionally, he has published on the business applications of AI, with papers introducing ContextBase as a future AI solution for companies and examining how ContextBase and large language models can solve complex business problems. These demonstrate his ability to connect advanced technology to real-world business needs.

John Akwei's publications cover a range of highly technical subject matter, evidencing his multifaceted expertise in data science, cutting-edge AI, physics, and translating these fields to practical business contexts. His role as lead author on these published works highlights his qualifications as an experienced professional and thought leader.

His reading interests include a wide variety of classic and contemporary fiction, non-fiction, and scientific texts. He has broad intellectual interests and a curious mind, with interests spanning literature, philosophy, psychology, science fiction, space exploration, mathematics, and more. He values learning about different subjects, time periods, and modes of thinking.

He enjoys philosophical and socially-conscious works that provide commentary on the human condition, as well as escapist genre fiction. He is open-minded, imaginative, and appreciates insightful observations on society and human nature. An interest in scientific topics, like space and mathematics, further indicates an analytic, inquisitive personality.
